Signing up for AOL email

Hello Auntie & Uncle!

In order to register new accounts at Facebook and Skype, you will need an existing email account to which you have access (i.e. you can read its emails).

As I understand it, Auntie wants to use a new email account from AOL to use for registering new accounts at Facebook and Skype.

Let's start with that, shall we?

1

To create a new AOL email account, open Internet Explorer on the bottom of your screen and go to https://mail.aol.com. Once there, notice that your screen will be similar to, but not identical to, this picture. You do not yet have an email account with AOL, so click 'Get a Free Username', which is circled with an arrow pointing to it. Note that your screen will have neither the red arrow nor will 'Get a Free Username' be circled in red.

2

Zoom

Next, we need to fill in most of the fields on this form. The information you see in the picture is for demonstration purposes; you'll want to fill in your own information (Auntie's).

Note the last two fields: Mobile phone & Alternate email. Only one of these fields is required. Mobile phone seems like it would be quicker, so the remainder of this guide presumes that that one was used to register. After filling out the rest of the form, type in your cell phone number (or any other phone number that can receive a text message and that you can check right away), then click the blue Sign Up button.

3

Zoom

Find the cell phone with the phone number you entered on the form.

There should be a text message (SMS) from AOL. This message will provide a 6-digit number that will need to be entered into the field on this form labeled Insert 6-digit code. If you haven't received the text yet, wait a few minutes. It might take a bit, but it should come through in no more than 24 hours.

Once it's typed in, click on the blue Finish button.

4

Zoom

If all went well, you should be at a screen similar to this.

Write down both the security question and security answer and keep it somewhere safe. It is required if you lose the password to this email account.

Click the blue Go to AOL Mail to go to your new email account!

5

Zoom

After clicking the blue Go to AOL Mail you may be taken to a screen similar to this one. Simply type in your new email address and password click the blue Sign In button.

6

Zoom

Right now, you should be in your new email account's Inbox.

You are now ready to register new accounts at Facebook and Skype using the email address you just created. To do so, just follow each respective link:

Facebook Signup: https://www.facebook.com/r.php

Join Skype: https://login.skype.com/join

If something didn't work right, try again. Let me know (1) which step had problems, (2) what you tried and (3) if there were any error messages, what the error messages said (verbatim, if possible). Email me at lowellv@me.com.
